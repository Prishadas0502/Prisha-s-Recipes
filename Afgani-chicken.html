Introduction

Afghani Chicken is a rich and creamy chicken dish known for its mild yet aromatic flavors. Marinated with yogurt, fresh cream, and mild spices, it has a luscious texture and smoky essence. It is typically grilled or tandoor-cooked and pairs well with naan or rice.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 500g bone-in chicken (or boneless)
  • ¬Ω cup thick yogurt

  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp cashew paste (soaked & blended cashews)
  • 1 tsp white pepper powder

  • ¬Ω tsp garam masala

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, fresh cream, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, cashew paste, white pepper, black pepper, garam masala, cumin, coriander powder, and salt.
  2. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Coat the chicken pieces with the marinade and let them rest for at least 3 hours (overnight for best results).
  3. Cook the Chicken:
    • Oven Method: Preheat oven to 200¬∞C (400¬∞F). Bake the marinated chicken for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway.
    • Grill/Tandoor Method: Grill on medium heat, basting with butter, until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Pan Method: Heat a little oil in a pan and cook on low flame until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Baste & Serve:
    • Brush with melted butter for extra flavor.
    • Serve hot with onion rings, lemon wedges, and mint chutney.
