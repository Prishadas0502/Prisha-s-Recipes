Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 25 mins | Servings: 4

Introduction

Aam Panna is a refreshing and hydrating Indian summer drink made from raw mangoes. It is sweet, tangy, and mildly spiced with cumin and black salt, making it a perfect thirst quencher. Known for its cooling properties, this traditional drink helps beat the summer heat while boosting digestion and immunity.

Ingredients

For the Aam Panna Base:

  • 2 large raw mangoes
  • 4 cups water
  • ¬Ω cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • ¬Ω tsp black salt (or regular salt)
  • ¬Ω tsp black pepper powder
  • 10-12 fresh mint leaves

For Serving:

  • 2 cups chilled water
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves & lemon slices (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Mangoes:
    • Wash the raw mangoes and boil them in water for 10-12 minutes until soft. Alternatively, you can roast them over an open flame for a smoky flavor.
    • Let them cool, then peel off the skin and scoop out the soft pulp.
  2. Prepare the Panna Base:
    • Blend the mango pulp with sugar, roasted cumin powder, black salt, black pepper, and mint leaves until smooth.
  3. Dilute and Mix:
    • Transfer the mixture to a large jug and add 2 cups of chilled water. Stir well.
  4. Serve:
    • Pour the Aam Panna into glasses over ice cubes. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and lemon slices.
