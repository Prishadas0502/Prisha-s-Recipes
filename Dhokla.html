Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins | Total Time: 30 mins | Servings: 4

Introduction

Dhokla is a popular and healthy steamed snack from Gujarat, India. Made with gram flour and fermented with yogurt or fruit salt, it is soft, spongy, and delicious. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or even as a side dish!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, water, lemon juice, ginger-chili paste, turmeric, sugar, and salt. Whisk until smooth.
  2. Activate the Batter: Just before steaming, add fruit salt (Eno) or baking soda, mix gently, and immediately pour the batter into a greased steaming tray.
  3. Steam the Dhokla: Steam for 15-20 minutes on medium heat until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let it cool slightly before cutting into pieces.
  4. Prepare the Tempering: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add water and sugar. Pour this tempering over the steamed dhokla.
  5. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander and grated coconut. Serve warm with green chutney!
