Introduction

Chicken Tikka is a delicious and smoky Indian appetizer made with marinated chicken pieces grilled to perfection. This dish is packed with bold flavors from yogurt, spices, and a hint of lemon, making it an all-time favorite. It‚Äôs perfect as a starter or can be used to make Chicken Tikka Masala!

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, coriander powder, garam masala, black salt, regular salt, and mustard oil.
    • Crush Kasuri methi between your palms and add it to the marinade. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Add chicken cubes to the marinade, coat them well, and let them rest for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  3. Cook the Chicken:
    • Oven Method: Preheat oven to 200¬∞C (400¬∞F). Place marinated chicken on skewers or a lined baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway.
    • Grill/Pan Method: Heat a grill pan or tandoor, brush with oil, and grill the chicken until cooked and slightly charred.
    • Tandoor Method: Cook over charcoal for an authentic smoky flavor.
  4. Baste & Serve:
    • Brush the cooked chicken with melted butter for extra juiciness.
    • Serve hot with onion rings, lemon wedges, and mint-coriander chutney.
